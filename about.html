About CareerPath

This project was built to give students a practical, honest overview of career choices after 10th, Inter, Diploma, and B.Tech. Content is concise by design and includes direct links to exams and colleges.

How to use
  • Start on the Home page → pick your current stage.
  • Open the Courses page for roadmaps and action items.
  • Check Colleges for quick official links.
  • Read Motivation when you need a reset.
