Management (BBA/BMS)

3-year undergrad in business fundamentals; can lead to MBA later. Good fit for entrepreneurship and corporate roles.

Typical Curriculum

  • Accounting & Finance Basics
  • Marketing & Sales
  • Operations & Supply Chain
  • Business Analytics
  • Organizational Behavior, HR

Entrance/Admissions

  • CUET-UG, IPMAT (IIM Indore/Rohtak)
  • Separate tests for certain private universities

Careers

  • Marketing/BD Associate
  • Operations/Analyst roles
  • Founding/Startup roles
  • MBA after 2–3 years of work

Representative Colleges

  • IIM Indore IPM
  • IIM Rohtak IPM
  • Delhi University
