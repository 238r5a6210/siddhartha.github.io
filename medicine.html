Medicine (MBBS)

5.5-year program including 1-year rotatory internship. Strong biology background required; NEET-UG is mandatory in India.

Typical Curriculum

  • Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry
  • Pathology, Microbiology, Pharmacology
  • ENT, Ophthalmology, Community Medicine
  • Medicine, Surgery, OBG, Pediatrics
  • Internship & clinical rotations

Entrance/Admissions

  • NEET-UG for MBBS/BDS
  • State counseling & All India Quota counseling
  • Private colleges have separate fee structures

Careers

  • Junior Doctor → Resident → Specialist (after PG)
  • Public health, research, medical administration

Representative Colleges

  • AIIMS New Delhi
  • CMC Vellore
  • JIPMER Puducherry
