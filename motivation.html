Motivation that actually helps

No clichés. Here are practical ideas when you feel stuck.

1% Rule

Improve one small thing daily: 30 minutes of focused study, one solved problem, one page of notes. Wins compound.

Portfolio > Marks

For tech/design, a solid portfolio beats raw scores. Build 3–5 small but real projects and write what you learned.

Systems > Goals

Set routines you can repeat. Example: 25‑min Pomodoro × 4 before lunch, practice questions in the evening.

Ask for Help

Seniors, teachers, alumni, LinkedIn — people help if you’re specific and respectful. Ask for feedback, not favors.

“Direction is more important than speed. Many are going fast nowhere.”
