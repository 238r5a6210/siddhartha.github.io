Design (B.Des)

User-centered program across product, communication, UI/UX, animation, etc. Portfolio-driven placements.

Typical Curriculum

  • Design Fundamentals, Drawing, Color
  • HCI & UX, Prototyping
  • Typography, Motion/Interaction
  • Studio Projects & Portfolio

Entrance/Admissions

  • UCEED (IITs) & NID DAT (NID)
  • Some private design schools have their own tests + portfolio interview

Careers

  • Product Designer, UI/UX Designer
  • Visual/Communication Designer
  • Motion/3D/Interaction Designer

Representative Colleges

  • IIT Bombay IDC
  • NID Ahmedabad
