After 10th
Streams & alternatives

Science (PCM/PCMB)

  • Ideal for engineering, medicine, research, data, aviation.
  • Focus: Math, Physics, Chemistry (+ Biology if medical).
Entrance: JEE Main/Advanced (Engg), NEET (Medical)
Next: Inter → Degrees

Commerce

  • Ideal for CA/CS/CMA, finance, management, entrepreneurship.
  • Focus: Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, Math (optional).
Entrance: CA Foundation, CUET UG
Explore: BBA, B.Com, Law

Arts/Humanities

  • Ideal for law, civil services, design, media, psychology, UPSC.
  • Focus: Pol. Sci., History, Sociology, Psychology, Languages.
Entrance: CUET UG, CLAT
Explore: BA, Design, Law

Polytechnic Diploma (3 yrs)

  • Practice-focused route to engineering and jobs.
  • Lateral entry to B.Tech (2nd year) via state CETs.
See Diploma roadmap

ITI / Skill Courses

  • Short, job‑oriented trades: Electrician, Fitter, Welder, IT, etc.
  • Great for early employment + government recruitment.
