Law (BA LL.B)

5-year integrated law program with internships and moots. Strong reading and reasoning skills needed.

Typical Curriculum

  • Legal Methods, Contracts, Torts
  • Constitutional & Criminal Law
  • IPR, Corporate, International Law
  • Moot Court, Internships

Entrance/Admissions

  • CLAT (for most NLUs)
  • AILET (for NLU Delhi)
  • Some state/private exams

Careers

  • Litigation, Corporate Law, In-house Counsel
  • Judicial Services (after LL.B + exam)

Representative Colleges

  • NLSIU Bengaluru
  • NALSAR Hyderabad
  • NLU Delhi
